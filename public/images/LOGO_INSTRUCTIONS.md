# Logo Setup Instructions

## Required Action

Please save your ScorpixMedia logo as:
```
/root/growprofile/public/images/logo.png
```

## Logo Specifications

- **Format**: PNG with transparent background (recommended)
- **Size**: 512x512px or larger (will be automatically resized)
- **Aspect Ratio**: Square (1:1)
- **Background**: Transparent or solid color

## Where the Logo Appears

The logo has been integrated throughout the application:

1. **Landing Page Navigation** - Top left with company name
2. **Footer** - Bottom of all pages
3. **Authentication Pages** - Login and Signup forms
4. **Dashboard Sidebar** - Left navigation panel
5. **Meta Tags** - For social media sharing

## Current Branding

- **Product Name**: GrowProfile
- **Company**: ScorpixMedia
- **Tagline**: Vision Made Visible

The logo will be displayed with the product name "GrowProfile" and "by ScorpixMedia" underneath in most locations.

## Next Steps

1. Copy the ScorpixMedia logo image to: `/root/growprofile/public/images/logo.png`
2. Refresh your browser to see the logo appear
3. Optional: Add favicon versions (see below)

## Optional: Favicon Setup

For complete branding, also add:
- `/root/growprofile/public/favicon.ico` (16x16, 32x32, 48x48)
- `/root/growprofile/public/apple-touch-icon.png` (180x180)
- `/root/growprofile/public/favicon-32x32.png`
- `/root/growprofile/public/favicon-16x16.png`
